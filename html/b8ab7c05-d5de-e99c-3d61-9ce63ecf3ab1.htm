Remarks
StripSize returns the equivalent size for a strip of data as it would be
            returned in a call to ReadEncodedStrip(Int32,  Byte , Int32, Int32) or as it would be expected in a
            call to WriteEncodedStrip .
            

            If the value of the field corresponding to ROWSPERSTRIP is
            larger than the recorded IMAGELENGTH, then the strip size is
            truncated to reflect the actual space required to hold the strip.
